# SmartLog Stakeholder Personas

**Purpose:** These stakeholder personas represent the decision-makers who will review and approve user stories for the SmartLog system.

---

## 1. Principal Pedro - School Director

### Profile
- **Name:** Pedro Reyes
- **Role:** School Principal / Director
- **Age:** 52
- **Experience:** 25 years in education, 10 years as principal
- **Tech Comfort:** Moderate - uses email, spreadsheets, basic apps

### Responsibilities
- Overall school operations and policy
- Parent and community relations
- Staff management and development
- Budget approval
- Regulatory compliance

### Primary Concerns
- Student safety and security
- Parent satisfaction and communication
- Staff efficiency and workload
- Cost-effectiveness
- Compliance with DepEd regulations

### Decision Style
- Conservative, prefers proven solutions
- Values simplicity over features
- Focuses on "will this help teachers teach?"
- Needs clear ROI justification

### Quote
> "Technology should make our teachers' lives easier, not add more work. And parents need to trust that their children are safe."

---

## 2. IT Manager Ivan - Technical Lead

### Profile
- **Name:** Ivan Santos
- **Role:** IT Manager / Systems Administrator
- **Age:** 35
- **Experience:** 12 years in IT, 5 years in education sector
- **Tech Comfort:** Expert - manages school network, servers, devices

### Responsibilities
- Network and server infrastructure
- System security and backups
- Device management (computers, scanners)
- Technical support for staff
- Vendor coordination

### Primary Concerns
- System security and data protection
- Infrastructure reliability (LAN-only is good)
- Maintenance and support burden
- Integration with existing systems
- Disaster recovery

### Decision Style
- Detail-oriented, asks technical questions
- Concerned about edge cases
- Wants clear documentation
- Prefers open standards

### Quote
> "I need to know this won't become a support nightmare. Can I troubleshoot it at 6 AM when something breaks?"

---

## 3. Registrar Rosa - Administrative Head

### Profile
- **Name:** Rosa Martinez
- **Role:** School Registrar / Admin Head
- **Age:** 45
- **Experience:** 20 years in school administration
- **Tech Comfort:** Good - uses student information systems daily

### Responsibilities
- Student enrollment and records
- Report generation for DepEd
- Parent communication
- ID card management
- Attendance compliance reporting

### Primary Concerns
- Data accuracy and integrity
- Report generation efficiency
- Ease of use for admin staff
- Bulk operations (enrollment season)
- Historical data preservation

### Decision Style
- Process-oriented, values consistency
- Concerned about training needs
- Wants features that save time
- Needs reliable exports for compliance

### Quote
> "During enrollment season, I process 500 students in two weeks. This system needs to handle that without crashing."

---

## 4. Parent Representative Patricia - PTA President

### Profile
- **Name:** Patricia Gonzales
- **Role:** PTA President / Parent Representative
- **Age:** 40
- **Experience:** 3 children in the school, 5 years in PTA
- **Tech Comfort:** Good - smartphone user, social media active

### Responsibilities
- Represent parent interests
- Communicate parent concerns to school
- Organize parent involvement activities
- Advocate for student welfare

### Primary Concerns
- Child safety and security
- Timely notifications about child
- Privacy of family information
- Ease of communication with school
- Cost passed to parents (if any)

### Decision Style
- Emotional, focuses on child welfare
- Values transparency
- Wants immediate notifications
- Concerned about privacy

### Quote
> "I just want to know my child arrived safely. A simple text message gives me peace of mind for the whole day."

---

## 5. Security Head Sergio - Facilities Manager

### Profile
- **Name:** Sergio Villanueva
- **Role:** Head of Security / Facilities Manager
- **Age:** 48
- **Experience:** Former police officer, 8 years in school security
- **Tech Comfort:** Basic - can use simple devices

### Responsibilities
- Gate security and access control
- Emergency response coordination
- Visitor management
- Guard supervision
- Safety drills

### Primary Concerns
- Quick identification of students
- Reliability of scanner devices
- Clear accept/reject feedback
- Handling of unknown persons
- Emergency lockdown procedures

### Decision Style
- Practical, wants foolproof systems
- Low tolerance for complexity
- Needs clear visual/audio feedback
- Values speed over features

### Quote
> "When 500 kids arrive in 30 minutes, I need this scanner to work every single time. No excuses."

| Decision Area | Pedro | Ivan | Rosa | Patricia | Sergio |
|--------------|-------|------|------|----------|--------|
| Overall Approval | **A** | C | C | I | I |
| Technical Architecture | I | **A** | I | - | I |
| User Interface/UX | C | I | **A** | C | C |
| Security Features | C | **A** | I | C | **R** |

| Scanner Operations | I | C | I | I | **A** |
